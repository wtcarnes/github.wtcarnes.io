Humidity


Humidity groups towards the 90th percentile because of the way the latitude and longitude is randomly generated in the jupyter notebook. CityPy locates the closest city to the generated coordinates. Given that the world is mostly water, most of the coordinates generated are in the ocean and the nearest city is coastal. This is what drives the Humidity towards the 90%
