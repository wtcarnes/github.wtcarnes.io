Cloudiness


Cloudiness is measured in a variety of ways surrounding okta values. Okta is a measurement of the sky cover on a scale of 1 to 8. There is also a measurement system based around METAR projections which are a series of tags that relate to certain conditions in the atmosphere. The reason for the aggregation around 20%, 40%,  75%, 100% is the traditional Okta measurements and the variant scatter can be related to METAR measurements.
